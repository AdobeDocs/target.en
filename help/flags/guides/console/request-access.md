# Request access {#request-access}

Access to Flags is managed at the team level. To use Flags, you need to be added to a team with the appropriate role.

## Prerequisites {#prerequisites}

Before requesting access, confirm the following:

* You know which team to join. Check with your product or engineering lead to identify your team and its admin.
* You know which sandbox you need access to.

## Request access {#request-steps}

Reach out to your Adobe representative for requesting access.

## Available roles {#roles}

The role assigned to you determines what actions you can perform in the console. Contact your admin if you are unsure which role to request.

| Role | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Admin** | Full access to the console. Can manage applications. |
| **Product Release Owner** | Can create and manage feature flags and feature groups, and release features to external users. |

## After access is granted {#after-access}

Once your request is approved, open Flags from within Adobe Target and verify that your applications are visible. See [Access Flags in Adobe Target](log-in-to-the-console.md) for next steps.

## Need help? {#need-help}

If you are unsure which team to join, or if no team exists for your project yet, contact your organization's Flags admin.
